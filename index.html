1

Stable coin

is a token, baked by CHF in Swiss banks.
Going to be launched in Q2-Q3, 2020

ROX Stable Coin will be backed by assets in special accounts of UBS and other partner banks, and equals to the Swiss Franc one to one. The ERC-20 token will be issued with every deposit of francs to the account instantly, and burned when the bank confirms withdrawal of funds on behalf of a customer. Banks inform the system in real time via a special secure protocol about the deposit of funds, at the same time the server sends this data to blockchain through a Smart Contract. As a result, the sender of funds receives tokens to the ETH address specified in a profile. All customers should pass KYC, AML and provide proof of funds. Third party Swiss VQF member will proceed all regulate agreements. 

Estimation of the launch Q2-Q3, 2020.
